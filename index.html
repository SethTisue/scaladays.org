CALL FOR PAPER

Speak at Scala Days

We are thrilled to announce the sixth annual Scala Days will be held this year at Fort Mason in San Francisco on March 16th through 18th, 2015. There will also be a Scala Days Europe at the Beurs Van Berlage in Amsterdam on June 8th through 10th, 2015. Now is the time to submit sessions and reserve the date on your calendar!

Scala Days brings together developers from all corners of the world to share their experiences and new ideas around creating applications with Scala, Akka and Play Framework. Scala Days provides a unique opportunity for Scala users to interact with the key contributors to the language and related technologies.

The call for papers is now open and will close on November 1, 2014. We're looking for creative ideas from you, so submit any topics you think the community would find exciting, inspiring, thought provoking, and interesting!

Please prepare an abstract of your talk (less than 1000 words) and submit your proposal.
