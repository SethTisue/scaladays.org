About Scala Days

Scala Days, the premier Scala Conference, makes its fifth annual debut on June 16th, 2014 at the Kosmos in Berlin.

The conference will bring together developers from all corners of the world to share their experiences and new ideas around creating applications with Scala and related technologies, like Akka and Play Framework. Scala Days provides a unique opportunity for Scala users to interact with the key contributors to the language and related technologies and connect with fellow developers.

Last year’s conference in New York city was a sold-out event at 500 attendees, and the buzz around Scala Days 2014 promises an even bigger show! Leaders from Scala User Groups and communities around the globe, students, and production users of Scala will gather to discuss academic research, enterprise use-cases and visionary projects for a two day, action-packed event.

Schedule

Scala Days 2014

Scala Days 2014 in Berlin will kick off on June 16th with a keynote by Scala creator, Martin Odersky, and featured speakers will include Jonas Bonér, Viktor Klang and others.

The call for papers is now closed. Stay tuned for the session lineup and schedule, coming soon! If you have any questions, please email info@scaladays.org
