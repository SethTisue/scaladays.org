CALL FOR PAPER

Speak at Scala Days

We are thrilled to announce the fifth annual Scala Days will be held this year in Kosmos Berlin on June 16th through 18th. Now is the time to submit sessions and reserve the date on your calendar!

Scala Days 2014 will bring together developers from all corners of the world to share their experiences and new ideas around creating applications with Scala, Akka and Play Framework. Scala Days provides a unique opportunity for Scala users to interact with the key contributors to the language and related technologies. This year’s keynote will be led by Martin Odersky, and featured speakers include Jonas Bonér, Viktor Klang and others.

The call for papers is now open and will close on November 29, 2013. We’re looking for creative ideas from you, so submit any topics you think the community would find exciting, inspiring, thought provoking, and interesting!

Please prepare an abstract of your talk (less than 1000 characters) and submit your proposal.
